Multiplayer: bots always play textbook basic strategy (no splitting, surrender, or insurance) and never wager — they're there purely to fill seats and consume the shoe realistically. When enabled, choosing a fresh shoe first asks how many bots to seat and where you'd like to sit before the cut-card prompt.

Double Down Madness: you start with just one card, can hit or double repeatedly in any order (doubling doesn't end your turn), no splitting — but if your first card is an ace, you get exactly one more card no matter what. Dealer busting on exactly 22 pushes your bets instead of paying them. Free Bet Blackjack: doubles on hard 9/10/11 and splits (except tens) cost nothing extra — in exchange, any dealer bust on exactly 22 pushes your remaining hands instead of paying them. Side bet paytables are common versions — casinos vary.
